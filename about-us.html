Enzo Brand's Boutique

Enzo Brand boutique started from the beginning of 2019 at Vushtrri located first. I had a strong desire to create a site where women of all ages, shapes, sizes and budgets could find their dream outfit. Not just the perfect dress, but the shoes, earrings, and purse needed to make it the outfit of your dreams. I knew I wanted to create fashion that was obtainable to everyone, because I believe that a woman's outfit strongly impacts how they feel. Everyone deserves to feel beautiful, and that is what I sought to create.

We currently have a staff of 41 employees (growing daily!), including my husband that selflessly left his mechanical engineering career to help grow the company alongside me. I am so thankful for what I get to do each and every day, and the team we have in place that I consider family is what makes our company the success that it is today. I am blessed by our staff immensely, and am so proud to call them mine!
